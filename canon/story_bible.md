## Story Premise
A debt-sunk obituary ghostwriter with acute memory loss discovers he has been pre-enrolled as the deceased client in a predatory funeral startup's system, forcing him to investigate who profits from declaring him dead.

## Story Purpose Statement
Use dark humor to examine how institutions monetize grief, precarity, and identity while following one man trying to remain morally human inside a bureaucracy built to process people as products.

## Thematic Spine
- theme_0001: Identity is political when institutions control your paperwork.
- theme_0002: Gallows humor can preserve dignity but cannot erase harm.
- theme_0003: Systems optimized for efficiency often cannibalize empathy.
