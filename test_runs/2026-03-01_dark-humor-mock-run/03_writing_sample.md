# Writing Sample (Persona Validation)

The kiosk asked Milo to confirm the spelling of his name for the memorial slideshow.

"I'd love to," he said, "but I don't remember owning one."

The screen flashed a sympathy animation: a candle, then two doves, then an upsell for premium grief lighting. Somewhere behind him, his landlord beat on the apartment door hard enough to qualify as percussion.

Milo selected **YES, I CONSENT TO MY LEGACY PACKAGE** because **NO, I'D PREFER NOT TO BE EVICTED** wasn't an option.

When the receipt printed, it listed him as both *Author* and *Late Mr. Milo Voss*.

"Great," he muttered, folding the paper into his pocket. "Even my obituary has a conflict of interest."
