# Chapter 01 Outline

## Chapter Purpose
A debt-sunk obituary ghostwriter wakes with no memory and discovers he has been pre-booked to write his own cheerful remembrance by the most predatory funeral startup in town; the chapter establishes his survival goal and the novel's darkly comic premise that monetized grief is just customer service with better flowers.

## Beats
- beat_01:
  - goal: Survive a bureaucratic morning without exposing total amnesia.
  - conflict/opposition: The funeral startup's intake AI insists he verify details of his own "upcoming service package," while his landlord pounds the door for rent.
  - outcome: He bluffs successfully, leaves with a contract he doesn't understand, and discovers the company has listed him as both employee and deceased client.
  - impacted_plots: [plot_0001]
- beat_02:
  - goal: Recover identity breadcrumbs before someone cashes in on his disappearance.
  - conflict/opposition: A former colleague recognizes him and blackmails him for access to sealed obituary archives.
  - outcome: He agrees to a temporary alliance and learns his missing memory may be tied to a falsified death report.
  - impacted_plots: [plot_0001]
