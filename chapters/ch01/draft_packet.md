# Chapter 01 Draft Packet

## Chapter Purpose
A debt-sunk obituary ghostwriter with amnesia learns he has been scheduled for his own funeral, forcing him to investigate who profits if he stays "dead."

## Beats and Ordered Scenes
1. **beat_01 → ch01_sc01**
   - Mandatory outcome: Milo exits the funeral startup with proof he is listed as deceased.
2. **beat_02 → ch01_sc02**
   - Mandatory outcome: Milo gains a lead that his death report was forged before his blackout.

## Open Questions (Non-blocking)

## Author Instructions
- **Tone:** Dark humor with sharp metaphors; jokes should expose fear, not erase it.
- **Pacing:** Fast scene starts, short reflective beats, escalating pressure at each scene end.
- **Stakes progression:** Financial ruin → identity erasure → potential homicide conspiracy.
- **Scene minimums:**
  - ch01_sc01 must establish world logic, Milo's voice, and immediate external threat.
  - ch01_sc02 must deepen mystery while binding Milo to a morally risky alliance.
